Why Use a Fictional Example?

  1. We wanted to give you something to work on that is outside of your day-to-day work. If you're new to brainstorming, hopefully this will make it easier to try it out.
  2. It helps us invent organizational strategies and goals that lend themselves to brainstorming.
  3. It gives you practice synthesizing input from different places.

Benefits of skits

  • Build empathy.
  • Focus on details of the experience.
  • Make abstract ideas concrete.
  • Provide an opportunity for stakeholder feedback.
